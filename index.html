Frequently Asked Questions

How does the offline mode work? +

QuickAid utilizes compressed SMS protocols to send location data when internet connectivity is low or unavailable.

Can I add multiple emergency contacts? +

Yes, you can add up to 5 trusted contacts who will receive an automated alert with your location the moment you trigger an emergency.
